## Making a Reservation
- Reserve online, by phone, or via our mobile app
- Reservations recommended for parties of **4 or more**
- Walk-ins welcome based on availability
- Large parties (8+): Please call to ensure proper seating arrangement

## Reservation Times
- Lunch: 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM
- Dinner: 5:00 PM - 9:30 PM (10:30 PM Fri/Sat)
- Weekend brunch: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM (Sat/Sun)

## Cancellation Policy

### Standard Reservations (1-7 guests)
- Cancel up to **2 hours** before reservation time
- No penalty for timely cancellations
- Cancellations within 2 hours or no-shows: **$25 per person fee** charged to card on file

### Large Parties (8+ guests)
- Cancel up to **24 hours** before reservation time
- Cancellations within 24 hours: **$35 per person fee**
- Credit card required to hold reservation
- Late arrivals (30+ minutes): Reservation may be released

### Private Dining & Events
- Requires signed contract and deposit
- Cancellation terms specified in contract
- Typically 7-14 day cancellation notice required
- Deposits non-refundable within 48 hours of event

## Arrival & Seating
- Please arrive **on time** for your reservation
- We will hold your table for **15 minutes** past reservation time
- After 15 minutes with no communication, table may be released
- Running late? Call us - we will do our best to accommodate

## Table Duration
- Tables are yours for **2 hours** for parties of 4 or fewer
- Larger parties and special occasions: **2.5 hours**
- If restaurant is not busy, you are welcome to stay longer

## Seating Requests
- Table preferences (booth, window, patio) are honored when possible
- Specific table requests cannot be guaranteed
- Indoor/outdoor seating subject to weather conditions
- High chairs and booster seats available upon request

## Party Size Changes
- Notify us **24 hours** in advance if party size increases
- We will do our best to accommodate
- Significant increases may require rescheduling
- Decreases in party size: Update reservation to avoid no-show fees

## Special Occasions
- Celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or special event?
- Let us know at booking - we love to help celebrate!
- Cake service: $25 cake-cutting fee if you bring your own
- Dietary restrictions: Please inform us at time of booking

## Dietary Accommodations
- Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options available
- Allergy information must be shared with server
- We cannot guarantee 100% allergen-free environment
- Severe allergies: Please call ahead to discuss options

## Dress Code
- Smart casual attire preferred
- No beachwear, athletic wear, or flip-flops for dinner service
- Lunch and brunch: Casual attire acceptable

## Children Welcome
- Family-friendly restaurant
- Children''s menu available
- High chairs and booster seats provided
- We ask parents to supervise children for safety and comfort of all guests
