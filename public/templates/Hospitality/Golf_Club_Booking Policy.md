## Booking Your Tee Time

### Advance Booking
- Members: Book up to **14 days** in advance
- Public: Book up to **7 days** in advance
- Online booking available 24/7 through our website or mobile app
- Phone bookings: Monday-Sunday, 7 AM - 6 PM

### Same-Day Tee Times
- Check availability online or call pro shop
- Walk-ins welcome based on availability
- Twilight rates available after 3 PM (seasonal)

## Cancellation Policy

### Individual Tee Times (1-3 players)
- Cancel up to **24 hours** before tee time: No penalty
- Cancellations within 24 hours: **$25 per player** no-show fee
- No-shows: **$50 per player** fee charged to card on file

### Group Outings (12+ players)
- Requires deposit of **$500** to hold date
- Cancel 14+ days before: Full refund minus $100 admin fee
- Cancel 7-14 days before: 50% refund
- Cancel less than 7 days: Deposit forfeited
- Rescheduling available once with 14+ day notice

### Tournament Reservations
- Signed contract and 50% deposit required
- Cancellation terms outlined in event contract
- Rain dates: Discussed at time of booking

## Check-In & Starting Times

### Arrival Time
- Arrive **30 minutes** before your tee time
- Check in at pro shop
- Warm up at practice facilities
- Late arrivals may forfeit tee time

### Pace of Play
- Expected pace: **4 hours and 15 minutes** for 18 holes
- Keep up with group ahead
- Rangers monitor pace and may ask slow groups to let others play through

### Starting Your Round
- Be at first tee **10 minutes** before your time
- Tee times run every **8-10 minutes**
- Late arrival (5+ minutes): May be paired with next group or rescheduled

## Weather Policy

### Rain/Inclement Weather
- Course remains open in light rain
- Course closes for lightning, heavy rain, or unsafe conditions
- Weather cancellations: Full refund or rain check issued
- Check weather alerts on our website/app

### Extreme Heat
- Course open in heat but play at your own risk
- Stay hydrated - water stations on course
- Consider early morning or twilight tee times

## Cart & Equipment Policies

### Golf Carts
- Included in green fee
- Cart-path-only rules may apply based on course conditions
- Valid driver''s license required for cart rental
- Cart damage: Player responsible for repair costs

### Pull Carts
- Available for rent: $10
- Walking encouraged (when not cart-path-only)
- Caddie service available by appointment

### Club Rentals
- Full sets available: $40
- Individual clubs: $10 each
- Reserve clubs when booking tee time

## Dress Code
- Collared shirts required (men)
- No denim jeans, cargo shorts, or athletic shorts
- Golf shoes or soft-spike athletic shoes
- Proper golf attire helps maintain course standards

## Guest Policy
- Members may bring up to **3 guests** per round
- Guest fees apply
- Guests must follow all club rules and dress code

## Practice Facilities
- Driving range open 7 AM - Dusk
- Range balls: $10 small bucket, $15 large bucket
- Putting green free for all players
- Short game area: Open to players with tee time or members only
