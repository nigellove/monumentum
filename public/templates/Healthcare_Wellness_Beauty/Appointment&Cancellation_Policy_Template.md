## Scheduling Appointments

### New Patients
- Please arrive **15 minutes early** to complete intake forms
- Bring photo ID and insurance card
- List of current medications recommended
- Intake forms available online for advance completion

### Established Patients
- Schedule via phone, patient portal, or mobile app
- Please arrive **5 minutes** early for check-in
- Update insurance information at each visit

### Appointment Types
- Consultations: 30 minutes
- Standard treatments: 45-60 minutes
- Extended procedures: 90+ minutes (varies by treatment)
- Emergency appointments: Call immediately for same-day scheduling

## Cancellation Policy

### Standard Cancellations
- **24-hour notice** required for cancellations
- Cancel via phone, patient portal, or mobile app
- No penalty for timely cancellations
- Cancellations within 24 hours: **$50 cancellation fee**

### Same-Day Cancellations
- Cancellations less than 24 hours: $50 fee charged to card on file
- Emergency situations evaluated on case-by-case basis
- Medical emergencies: Fee may be waived (documentation may be required)

### No-Show Policy
- No-shows without notification: **$75 fee** per appointment
- Fee charged to credit card on file
- Second no-show: May be discharged from practice
- Outstanding balances must be paid before scheduling future appointments

### Repeated Cancellations
- Three late cancellations in 12 months: May impact ability to schedule
- Pattern of late cancellations: May require prepayment or deposit for future appointments

## Rescheduling

### Advance Rescheduling
- Reschedule with 24+ hours notice: No fee
- Reschedule within 24 hours: $25 fee
- Same-day rescheduling treated as cancellation

### Appointment Flexibility
- Preferred appointment times cannot always be guaranteed for rescheduled appointments
- We will accommodate your schedule whenever possible

## Late Arrivals

### Patient Running Late
- Call immediately if you will be late
- Arrivals up to **10 minutes late**: Seen when possible
- Arrivals 10-15 minutes late: May need to reschedule
- Arrivals 15+ minutes late: Treated as no-show

### Provider Running Late
- We will notify you if provider is running behind
- Wait time exceeding 20 minutes: Option to reschedule with no fee
- We apologize for delays and appreciate your patience

## Insurance & Payment

### Insurance Verification
- Insurance verified before appointment
- Co-pays due at time of service
- You are responsible for deductibles and non-covered services
- Insurance claim denials: Patient responsibility

### Payment Required
- Payment or payment arrangements required before treatment
- Accepted: Cash, check, credit/debit cards, HSA/FSA cards
- Payment plans available for treatments over $500
- Outstanding balances over 60 days: May be sent to collections

### Treatment Estimates
- Cost estimates provided before beginning treatment
- Final cost may vary based on actual treatment needed
- Unexpected findings during treatment will be discussed before proceeding
