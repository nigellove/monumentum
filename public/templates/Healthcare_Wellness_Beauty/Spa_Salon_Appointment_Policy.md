## Booking Your Appointment

### How to Book
- Online booking available 24/7
- Call us during business hours
- Mobile app available for iOS and Android
- Walk-ins welcome based on stylist availability

### First-Time Clients
- Consultation included with first appointment
- Arrive 10 minutes early for consultation
- Bring photos of desired style/color
- Patch test required 48 hours before first color service

### Regular Clients
- Book your next appointment before leaving
- Recurring appointments can be pre-scheduled
- Preferred stylist can be requested (subject to availability)

## Cancellation & No-Show Policy

### Advance Cancellations
- **24-hour notice** required for cancellations
- Cancel online, via app, or by phone
- No fee for cancellations with proper notice
- Rescheduling within 24 hours: No fee if new appointment within 7 days

### Late Cancellations
- Cancellations within 24 hours: **50% of service cost** charged to card on file
- Same-day cancellations: **Full service cost** charged
- Medical emergencies: Contact us to discuss waiver

### No-Show Policy
- No-shows without notice: **Full service cost** charged
- Credit card required to book appointment
- Two no-shows: Future appointments require prepayment
- Three no-shows: May be unable to book future appointments

## Late Arrivals

### If You're Running Late
- Call immediately if running late
- Up to 10 minutes late: Full service provided if time permits
- 10-15 minutes late: Service may be shortened (full price applies)
- 15+ minutes late: Appointment may be rescheduled (late cancellation fee applies)

### Out of Respect for Other Clients
- Late arrivals may result in abbreviated service to stay on schedule
- We cannot extend appointment beyond scheduled time if you arrive late
- Full price charged regardless of service time reduction

## Color Services - Important Information

### Patch Test Required
- New color clients: Patch test **48 hours** before appointment
- Book patch test when scheduling color service
- Patch test free of charge
- Cannot perform color service without successful patch test

### Color Consultation
- Consultation free with service
- Bring inspiration photos
- Realistic expectations: Some colors require multiple sessions
- Color correction: May require extended appointment and additional cost

### Color Aftercare
- Specialized shampoo/conditioner recommended (available for purchase)
- Wait 48 hours before washing hair after color
- Avoid chlorine/salt water for 7 days
- Touch-up appointments typically every 6-8 weeks

## Service Guarantees

### Satisfaction Guarantee
- Not happy with your service? Let us know within **7 days**
- Complimentary adjustment appointment
- Original stylist will make corrections at no charge
- Cannot guarantee specific results for color corrections from other salons

### What's Not Covered
- Color fading due to improper aftercare
- Heat damage from excessive home styling
- Changes in hair texture from chemical treatments elsewhere
- Unrealistic expectations discussed during consultation
