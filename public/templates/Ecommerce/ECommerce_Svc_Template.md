- Refunded to original payment method
- Original shipping costs are **non-refundable** (unless item is defective)

- Perishable goods (food, flowers, plants)
- Personal care items that have been opened
- Custom or personalized products
- Clearance/final sale items (marked as such)
- Gift cards or downloadable products
- Intimate apparel or swimwear (if hygiene seal is broken)

- If item arrives defective or incorrect, we cover return shipping
