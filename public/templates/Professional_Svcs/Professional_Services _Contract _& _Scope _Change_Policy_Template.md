## Engagement Terms

### Project Scope
- All projects begin with signed Statement of Work (SOW)
- SOW defines: Deliverables, timeline, milestones, pricing
- Project cannot begin without executed agreement
- Changes to scope require written amendment

### Payment Terms
- **50% deposit** required to begin work
- Remaining balance due upon project completion
- Monthly retainer projects: Billed first of each month
- Payment due within **15 days** of invoice date

### Late Payment
- Grace period: 5 business days
- Late fee: **1.5% per month** (18% annual) on overdue balance
- After 30 days: Work may be paused until payment received
- After 60 days: Collection proceedings may begin

## Project Timeline

### Start Date
- Project begins upon receipt of signed SOW and deposit
- Kickoff meeting scheduled within 5 business days
- Client deliverables (content, access, etc.) required for progress

### Delays
- Delays caused by client: Timeline extended accordingly, no cost adjustment
- Delays caused by us: Timeline extended, potential discount discussed
- Force majeure: Timeline adjusted, no penalty to either party

### Milestone Approvals
- Client has **5 business days** to review and approve each milestone
- Feedback must be specific and actionable
- Delays in approval extend project timeline
- After 3 rounds of revisions at milestone, additional revisions billed hourly

## Scope Changes

### Change Request Process
1. Client submits change request in writing
2. We assess impact on timeline and budget
3. Change order created with updated terms
4. Client approves change order in writing
5. Work proceeds on revised scope

### Pricing for Changes
- Minor changes (less than 2 hours): Included in project
- Moderate changes: Hourly rate ($150/hour) applies
- Major changes: New SOW with updated fixed price
- Rush changes: 50% premium on hourly rate

### Timeline Impact
- All scope changes extend timeline unless rush service requested
- Client must approve timeline extension before work proceeds
- Original deadline maintained only if scope reduced elsewhere

## Deliverables & Revisions

### Initial Deliverables
- Delivered according to SOW timeline
- Include specified rounds of revisions (typically 2)
- Revisions must be requested within 10 business days

### Revision Rounds
- Round 1: Comprehensive changes allowed
- Round 2: Minor refinements only
- Round 3+: Billed at hourly rate ($150/hour)
- Revisions do not include scope changes (requires change order)

### Final Approval
- Client has **10 business days** to provide final approval
- After approval, project considered complete
- Additional changes billed as new work

## Cancellation & Termination

### Client Cancellation
- Client may terminate engagement with **30-day written notice**
- Deposit non-refundable
- Payment due for all completed work
- Partial milestone work billed at hourly rate
- Client receives all completed deliverables

### Agency Termination
- We may terminate engagement with 30-day notice if:
  - Client repeatedly misses payment deadlines
  - Client fails to provide required materials/access
  - Scope creep becomes unmanageable despite change orders

### Immediate Termination
- Either party may terminate immediately for material breach
- Material breach examples: Non-payment after 60 days, violation of contract terms
- Termination does not waive payment for work completed

## Warranties & Limitations

### Quality Guarantee
- Work performed to professional industry standards

## Retainer Agreements

### Monthly Retainer Terms
- Minimum 3-month commitment
- Hours: 20, 40, or 80 hours per month (package dependent)
- Unused hours do not roll over
- Additional hours billed at standard hourly rate
- 60-day notice required to cancel retainer

### Retainer Deliverables
- Scope defined monthly based on available hours
- Priority given to urgent requests
- Non-urgent work scheduled based on capacity
