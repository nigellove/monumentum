## Subscription Plans

### Free Trial
- **14-day free trial** - no credit card required
- Full access to all features during trial
- Upgrade anytime during trial period
- Trial automatically expires (no auto-billing)

### Monthly Subscription
- Billed monthly on subscription date
- Cancel anytime - no long-term commitment
- Access continues through end of billing period
- Price: $49/month per user

### Annual Subscription
- Billed annually (20% savings vs monthly)
- Price: $470/year per user ($39.17/month equivalent)
- Commitment: 12 months
- Auto-renews unless cancelled 30 days before renewal

## Billing & Payment

### Payment Methods
- Credit card, debit card, PayPal
- ACH/bank transfer available for annual plans
- Invoicing available for 10+ user accounts

### Billing Cycle
- Charged on same day each month/year
- Automatic renewal unless cancelled
- Billing date adjustable upon request

### Failed Payments
- 3 retry attempts over 7 days
- Email notifications sent after each failed attempt
- Account suspended after final failed attempt
- Reactivation: Update payment method and pay outstanding balance

## Upgrades & Downgrades

### Upgrading Your Plan
- Upgrade anytime from account settings
- Pro-rated credit applied to new plan
- New features available immediately
- Billing adjusts on next cycle

### Downgrading Your Plan
- Downgrade takes effect at end of current billing period
- Data retained but feature access limited per new plan
- Downgrade from annual to monthly: Takes effect after annual term

### Adding/Removing Users
- Add users anytime - prorated charge for current period
- Remove users effective next billing cycle
- Minimum 1 user per account

## Cancellation Policy

### How to Cancel
- Cancel anytime from account settings under "Billing"
- Or email support: cancel@company.com
- Cancellation confirmation sent via email
- No cancellation fees

### Monthly Subscriptions
- Cancel anytime
- Access continues until end of current billing period
- No refunds for partial months
- Data accessible for 30 days after cancellation

### Annual Subscriptions
- Cancel anytime but no refunds after 30 days
- First 30 days: Full refund if dissatisfied
- After 30 days: No refund for remaining months
- Access continues through end of annual term

### Reactivation
- Reactivate anytime within 90 days of cancellation
- Data fully restored
- After 90 days: Account reactivation requires new setup

## Refund Policy

### Money-Back Guarantee
- **30-day money-back guarantee** for new subscribers
- Full refund if not satisfied
- No questions asked
- Email support: refunds@company.com

### After 30 Days
- Monthly plans: No refunds for current month
- Annual plans: No refunds for remaining term
- Exception: Service outages exceeding 48 hours

### Pro-rated Refunds
- Not available except in cases of extended service outage
- Downgrades: No refund, change takes effect next cycle

## Data & Account After Cancellation

### Data Export
- Export all data anytime before cancellation
- Formats: CSV, JSON, PDF
- Data export tool available in account settings

### Data Retention
- Data retained for **90 days** after cancellation
- Reactivate anytime to restore data
- After 90 days: Data permanently deleted (per GDPR/CCPA)

### Account Deletion
- Request permanent account deletion anytime
- All data deleted within 30 days of request
- Cannot be undone

## Auto-Renewal

### How It Works
- Subscription auto-renews at end of each period
- Notification sent **7 days** before renewal
- Cancel before renewal date to avoid charge

### Opting Out
- Turn off auto-renewal in account settings
- Account remains active through current period
- Manual renewal required to continue service

- 25+ users: Custom pricing available
