Control Your Agent's Personality

Your AI agent learns how to respond based on the settings you configure during setup. Here's what you can control:

Personalized Greeting

Set the first message visitors see when they open the chat. This is crucialâ€”it tells them what your business does.

  • Generic: "Hi! How can I help?"
  • Better: "Hi! Need help with HVAC repair or maintenance?"
  • Best: "Hi! Looking for commercial HVAC solutions? We specialize in complex industrial systems."

A specific greeting immediately qualifies visitors and sets expectations.

Tone of Voice

Choose how your agent communicates. This affects word choice, sentence structure, and overall feel:

  • Professional: Formal, detail-oriented, business language
  • Friendly: Warm, conversational, approachable
  • Casual: Relaxed, fun, informal

The tone you choose will be applied throughout the entire conversation.

Business Overview

Describe what you do in 2-3 sentences. The AI uses this to personalize responses:

  • What you do: "We provide HVAC repair and maintenance"
  • Who you serve: "for commercial office buildings"
  • What makes you different: "with same-day emergency service"

Example: "Peak Home Services provides emergency HVAC repair and maintenance for commercial buildings in the tri-state area. We specialize in same-day response for critical systems."

Business Hours

Tell your agent when you're available. The AI uses this to set expectations:

  • "Mon-Fri 8am-6pm, Sat 9am-1pm"
  • "24/7 emergency service available"
  • "Response within 2 hours during business hours"

Best Practices

ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

  • Be specific in your greeting: "Hi! Need help with industrial HVAC?" outperforms "Hi! How can I help?"
  • Match your tone to your audience: B2B = professional. D2C = friendly.
  • Describe your business clearly: "Plumbing repair" is less effective than "Licensed plumbing repair for residential homes in Chicago."
  • Request only essential info: Asking for 10 fields drops conversion 60%. Ask for name + email first, then expand.
  • Update your hours accurately: If your agent says you're open 9-5 but it's 6pm, visitors get frustrated.
