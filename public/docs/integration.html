How to manage your captured leads and connect them to your workflow.

How Leads Are Captured

Every time a visitor has a conversation with your AI agent, a lead is automatically created:

  1. Visitor starts a chat
  2. AI asks qualifying questions
  3. Visitor provides their info (name, email, phone, company, details about their need)
  4. Conversation is captured in real-time
  5. Email notification sent to you
  6. Lead stored in your dashboard

‚úÖ What You Get in Each Lead

  • Name: Visitor's name (extracted from conversation)
  • Email: Their email address
  • Phone: Contact number (if provided)
  • Company: Their company name (if provided)
  • Message: Summary of what they said
  • Full Transcript: Complete conversation history
  • Lead Score: AI qualification (hot/warm/cold)
  • Sentiment: Were they satisfied? frustrated?
  • Timestamp: When the conversation happened

View Your Leads

Log in to your Monumentum dashboard to see all your leads:

  1. Go to monumentum.ai/dashboard
  2. Navigate to "Leads" section
  3. View all conversations with details
  4. Search by name, company, or date
  5. Sort by lead score (hottest leads first)

Email Notifications

‚úÖ Available Now

Every new lead triggers an email to your inbox with:

  • Visitor name and contact info
  • Lead qualification score (hot/warm/cold)
  • Summary of their need
  • Full conversation transcript
  • Link to respond or follow up

Timing: Email arrives within 1-2 minutes of the conversation ending.

Export Your Leads

‚úÖ Available Now

Download your leads anytime as a CSV file:

  1. Go to your dashboard
  2. Click "Leads" section
  3. Click "Export as CSV"
  4. Open in Excel, Google Sheets, or your CRM

CSV includes: Name, Email, Phone, Company, Message Summary, Lead Score, Captured Date, Full Transcript

Using Exported Leads

Once you have the CSV, you can:

  • Import into HubSpot, Salesforce, or Pipedrive
  • Follow up manually with your sales team
  • Sort by lead score to prioritize hottest prospects
  • Analyze trends in a spreadsheet
  • Share with your team

Weekly Analytics Report

‚úÖ Available Now

Every Sunday morning, receive an email with your week's analytics:

  • Total conversations: How many people chatted with your agent
  • Lead breakdown: Hot/Warm/Cold leads count
  • Top topics: What customers asked about most
  • Sentiment trends: Were customers happy or frustrated?
  • Top 3 action items: Recommendations to improve conversion

Note: Analytics are sent Sunday morning for the previous week (Mon-Sun).

Best Practices

üí° How to Get the Most Value

  • Follow up fast: Respond to hot leads within 1 hour while they're warm
  • Review your weekly analytics: Understand what customers are really asking for
  • Refine your greeting: If many leads are cold, adjust your greeting to better qualify
  • Export leads weekly: Even before auto-sync, download and import to your CRM
  • Check sentiment trends: If satisfaction drops, something might be wrong with your agent
