What Happens When Someone Visits

Here's the actual flow:

  1. Visitor lands on your page
  2. Chat widget appears in the corner
  3. They click to start a conversation
  4. Your AI agent welcomes them with your personalized greeting
  5. AI asks qualifying questions (who you are, what you need, budget, timeline, etc.)
  6. Conversation is captured in real-time
  7. You get an email notification with the complete conversation
  8. Lead appears in your dashboard

Troubleshooting

If you don't see the widget on your site:

  • Verify you pasted the code before </body> (not in <head>)
  • Make sure your customer ID is correct (check your welcome email)
  • Clear your browser cache and do a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R or Cmd+Shift+R)
  • Check your browser console for JavaScript errors (F12 â†’ Console tab)
  • Wait 2-3 minutes for the widget to load on first installation
