Cookie Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Monumentum uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (https://www.monumentum.ai). It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enable core site functionality (e.g., navigation, page loading)
  • Understand how visitors use our website and improve performance
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Remember your preferences or login state (if applicable)

We do not use cookies to collect personal data without your consent, nor do we sell or share cookie data for advertising purposes.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (cannot be disabled).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences or settings to enhance experience.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can usually configure your browser to block or delete cookies, but this may impact some site features or functionality.

To learn more, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, law, or our business practices. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
